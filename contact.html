OpenPGP

If you have no idea what these mean, read the Email Self Defense tutorial.

Usually, electronic mail that I send are signed with my OpenPGP key. Please use OpenPGP when contacting me through electronic mail. The following are available.

  • My ECC key (recommended)
  • My RSA key
  • My keyring of friends

In order to verify the authenticity of the key, check if you've actually downloaded these keys from andrewyu.org, and also check the fingerprints with me elsewhere (email, IRC, in real life, etc) before trusting or signing it.

Once confirmed, please sign and share my key in order to build the OpenPGP Web of Trust.
